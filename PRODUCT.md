# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

DC Metro small business owners, $800K–$4M revenue. Three verticals: (1) cafes, restaurants, hotel/B&B owners losing revenue to commission platforms or stalling post-launch; (2) real estate and home services fighting generic franchise marketing; (3) creative professionals and boutique agencies with great work but a quiet feed. They're comparison-shopping agencies, skeptical of overpromising, and arrive from social or search. Mobile and desktop split.

## Product Purpose

Marketing website for Nyoo Studio — a two-founder creative marketing agency (Jin and Jo) operating DC to Richmond corridor. Primary conversion: get qualified visitors to book a strategy call. Secondary: establish credibility and signal selectivity. The site is also proof of work — if the site looks sharp, it demonstrates what Nyoo does for clients.

## Brand Personality

Sharp-eyed, All-in, Relentless. Secondary traits: Cinematic, After-hours cool, Selective, Dangerous competence. Warm underneath directness, never on top of it. Speaks to potential, never to fear.

## Anti-references

- Generic Webflow agency sites (bold sans headline, cream/beige background, floating section cards, "We help brands grow" energy)
- Squarespace template feel (centered layouts, stock photo grids, muted pastels, soft everything)
- AI-generated website aesthetics (gradient text, glassmorphism cards, hero-metric templates, identical icon+heading+text card grids, neon-on-black)
- Performed enthusiasm — exclamation marks, emoji strings, "We're passionate about..."
- Vanity metric lead ("10,000 posts created!") over outcome metrics

## Design Principles

1. **The site is the proof.** Every design choice is a client pitch. If it looks generic, it undermines the sales argument.
2. **Earned darkness.** The midnight palette works because it's intentional, not lazy dark mode. Color behaves like light — it emanates, it doesn't fill.
3. **Selectivity as design.** The agency is choosy about clients. The site should feel like it doesn't need you — that tension earns trust.
4. **Declarative confidence.** No hedging copy, no explaining, no selling on anxiety. Show the outcome; let the visitor decide.
5. **Outcome over feature.** Client results, not deliverable lists. Proof over adjectives.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Reduced motion respected (prefers-reduced-motion already implemented in Hero). No specific color blindness or additional accommodation requirements beyond AA contrast ratios.
